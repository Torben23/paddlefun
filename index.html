What are the lesson prices?

  • For duos, enjoy our couple's special at 25 USD.

Lessons attendance requires prior reservation.

Lesson Schedule:

  • Beginners: 10 AM - 11 AM.
  • Beginners Intermediate: 11 AM - 12 PM
  • Intermediate: 12 PM - 01 PM.

What should I bring to the lessons?

  • Comfortable swimwear.
  • Sunscreen
  • A hat for sun protection.
  • Sunglasses with a strap.
  • Waterproof camera (optional)

We provide the paddleboard and safety gear.

Is a life jacket required during the lessons?

  • Yes, for your safety, all participants must agree to use the provided life jackets during the lesson, safety is our top priority.

Can I have my experience filmed?

  • Absolutely! For an additional fee, we offer a drone videography service to capture your paddleboarding adventure. Capture your paddleboard journey on Lake Atitlan from a unique aerial perspective!
